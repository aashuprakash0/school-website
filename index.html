About Us

Shaping Future Leaders Since Day One

Aadarsh Avasiya School is a premier English medium boarding school committed to providing quality education. We combine academic rigour with character development, equipping every student with the skills, confidence, and values to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our campus offers state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated faculty, and a vibrant co-curricular programme that ensures every child discovers and develops their unique potential.

Director's Message

A Message From Our Director

Education is not merely the accumulation of knowledge — it is the illumination of the mind, the awakening of potential, and the shaping of character. At Aadarsh Avasiya School, we have built more than an institution; we have built a community where every child is seen, heard, and nurtured.

Our dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and values-based curriculum work together to ensure that each student not only excels academically but also grows into a responsible, compassionate, and confident individual. We believe that the years a child spends in school are the most formative of their life — and we take that responsibility seriously.

I invite every parent and student to become part of the Aadarsh family. Together, we will build a brighter tomorrow.

— Kedar Prasad Sah
